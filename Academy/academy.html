DESCRIPTION

Meca Academy is the first of its kind to take place in the faculty of engineering ASU, the first academy was in 2007 The academy intends to provide real life simulation to the business world observed in any of the leading companies in Egypt. Sessions are conducted by experts from the company’s different departments.
As all our events must hit our mission by a way or another, Meca Academy represents a part of a business functions. In each module, after the sessions, the participants apply what they have learnt in a case study where they are divided into teams, each one representing a company with its departments. Consequently, they work on a project which is later presented in front of the company’s managers.
