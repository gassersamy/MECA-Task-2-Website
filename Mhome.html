ABOUT US

MECA is a non-profit student to student activity founded in 2005 and approved by the faculty dean.
MECA stands for Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, and Architecture (the four majors in faculty of engineering ASU, Cairo, and Egypt.

CORE VALUES

Respect
Added value
Appreciation
Professionalism

MISSION

Improve students' technical and non-technical skills through professional trainers.

VISION

Qualify students from all majors for the job market's needs, in order to have a better career.

MECA EVENTS

Academy

The Academy intended to provide real life simulation to the business world observed in any of the leading companies in Egypt which was: Schneider Electric and ExxonMobil.

Enterprise

The event aim to improve students' technical skills and to decrease the gap between students and the market needs.

The event is versatile consisting of : internships, case study, sessions and site visits according to the students' preferences.

MECA Venture

MECA Venture is an event that aims to enhance in students technical and non-technical skills, it takes place in the second term in Cairo University. The event hits its mission by a sessions which is conducted by experts from Multinational companies.

MECAzine

MECAzine's vision is to increase the awareness of its readers and transfer MECA's culture through all types of articles as Human Development articles,entertainment articles , and also sponsors and commercial Ads.

MECA Seminars

MECA seminars are a set of seminars held at the faculty and serves technical and nontechnical topics.

MECA Employment Fair

It is an event that gives the chance for undergraduate students to meet the representatives from different multinationals to understand more about the working field.
